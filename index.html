MVP==
MVP features for product Ola Cab Sanitization.
1. Sanitization tab shows cab is sanitized or not after every
single ride. Disinfect cars before every single trip as part of
safety standards for riders and drivers. Verified cab sanitization
option should be displayed by sanitization center head.
2. Driver Wearing mask and temperature Check After accepting
the ride driver should upload a picture with wearing mask and
body temperature should display on app so customer will have
sense of satisfaction and acceptance.
3. Vaccination Status & Certificate Vaccination status tab will
show driver fully partially vaccinated or not. Also contain driver
vaccination certificate which will reflect whether driver has
taken only 1st dose of vaccination or both the dose.
4. Isolation white thin shield cover should be present between
front and back seat. Sanitization center head should inspect the
cab and make sure its properly isolated covered by shield and
update same status verified on the app.
5. Cashless payment Payment should be completely cashless
through UPI Paytm gpay phonepe. There should be option to
notify if driver asking for cash or cancels cab as driver want
cash payment.
